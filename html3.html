COVID-19

Coronavirus disease(COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus

The disease causes respiratory illness(like a flu) with symptoms such as cough. fever and in more severe cases, difficulty breathing. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face and avoiding close contact(1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell

How it Spreads

Coronavirus disease spread primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a surface or obejct that has the virus on it, then touches their eyes, nose or mouth
